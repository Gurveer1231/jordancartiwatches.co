ABOUT

Jordan Carti Watches.Co is a newly founded business that is run by CEO Gurveer Kharyal. Our watches are more than just a clock on your wrist, but a fashion statement 100% eco-friendly and affordable to all!

We as designers and creators of these watches focus on the art of finding eco-friendly and sustainable materials that can be implemented in our watches.
02
DONATIONS

Jordan Carti Watches.Co focuses on the development of eco-friendly watches. Pollution is very common, especially with e-waste, so we have decided to partner with the Habitat for Humanity.

Donate or choose to bring in laptops, watches, phones, the Habitat for Humanity recycles and reuses e-waste and other polluting materials. We decided to partner with the Habitat for Humanity due to the fact that the environment is something we deeply cherish and care about.
